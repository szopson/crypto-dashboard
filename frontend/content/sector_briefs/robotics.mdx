**Thesis.** Robotics and automation are entering a secular growth phase driven by persistent labor shortages, falling hardware costs, and the fusion of AI/computer vision that finally delivers on autonomous mobile robot promises. Warehouse automation is mature and scaling; surgical robotics are seeing payor acceptance broaden; industrial cobot adoption is inflecting in mid-market manufacturing. Valuations have reset from 2021 peaks but fundamentals are two years ahead of where the market prices them.

**Where we are in the cycle.** Early-to-mid expansion. Warehouse automation vendors are in harvest mode with strong unit economics; surgical and industrial cobots are crossing the chasm from early adopters to pragmatists. Not yet euphoric—public comps trade 3-5x forward sales vs. 10x+ in 2021.
