**Thesis.** Grid-scale storage deployments are accelerating rapidly as renewables penetration hits inflection points globally, but the sector is trapped in a brutal pricing war. Chinese lithium oversupply has cratered battery costs 40% YoY, squeezing integrator margins while tariffs fragment supply chains. Winners will be verticals with proprietary tech (long-duration, thermal) or locked-in utility contracts; commodity lithium-ion assemblers face a bloodbath through 2027.

**Where we are in the cycle.** Mid-cycle shakeout. Demand visibility is excellent but the industry overbuilt capacity 2023-2025, and we're now in a margin trough. Expect 30-40% of marginal players to exit or consolidate by late 2027; survivors emerge with pricing power as supply rationalizes.
