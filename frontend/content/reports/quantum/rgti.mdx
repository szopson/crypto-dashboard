## Verdict

**Macro context.** The quantum computing sector is navigating a ~20% pullback as investors demand tangible commercial progress to justify triple-digit multiples, while Trump's June 2026 executive orders provide a structural policy tailwind for U.S. players. A dovish rate path would support long-duration speculative names, but the market is increasingly bifurcating toward profitable infrastructure plays.
