## Verdict

**Macro context.** The AI-application trade remains the market's dominant theme, and the FY27 defense budget's autonomous-warfare push is a direct tailwind for Palantir's government core. However, high-multiple growth names carry elevated sensitivity to any hawkish rate repricing or risk-off rotation.

Palantir is a genuinely exceptional business — 84.7% growth, 46% operating margins, fortress balance sheet, and a perfect beat record make it the premier public AI-operationalization play. But at 61x sales and 156x EV/EBITDA, with a 0.54% FCF yield, net insider selling of $25.5M, and price stuck below its 200-day, the risk-reward is symmetric at best. We rate HOLD with a $155 target and would accumulate aggressively only on a pullback toward the $106-115 support zone.
